Templates

Copy-Paste Prompts

50+ battle-tested prompts. Click copy, paste into ChatGPT, and get instant expert results.

Writing

📝 Blog Post from Topic

"Write a comprehensive blog post about [TOPIC]. Target audience: [AUDIENCE]. Tone: [TONE]. Include: an engaging introduction, 5 main sections with subheadings, a practical example, and a conclusion with a call-to-action. Word count: 1200-1500."

💡 Works for any niche — just fill in the brackets.

Coding

💻 Code Review & Debug

"Review the following code for bugs, security vulnerabilities, and performance issues. Explain each problem and provide a corrected version. Also suggest 2-3 improvements for readability and efficiency. [ paste your code here ]"

💡 Paste 50-200 lines for best results.

Business

📊 Market Research Summary

"Analyze the [INDUSTRY] market. Provide: 1) Market size and growth rate, 2) Top 5 competitors with strengths/weaknesses, 3) Key trends for 2025-2026, 4) Opportunities for new entrants, 5) Risks to watch. Format as a structured report with bullet points."

💡 Great for pitch decks and business plans.

Learning

🎓 Explain Like I'm 5

"Explain [COMPLEX TOPIC] as if I'm a 12-year-old. Use analogies, simple language, and a real-world example. Then provide a slightly more advanced version for a college student. Keep both under 300 words."

💡 Best way to truly understand any topic.

Marketing

📢 Ad Copy Generator

"Write 5 ad variations for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Target audience: [AUDIENCE]. Platform: [Facebook/Google/LinkedIn]. Each ad must include: a hook (first 5 words), a benefit, social proof, and a CTA. Max 100 words per ad."

💡 A/B test the top 2 performers.

Career

💼 Resume Bullet Rewriter

"Rewrite these resume bullets to be more impactful. Use strong action verbs, quantifiable results, and industry keywords. Format each as: 'Accomplished [X] by doing [Y], resulting in [Z metric].' [ paste bullets here ]"

💡 Quantify everything — numbers get interviews.

AI

🤖 Chain-of-Thought Reasoning

"Solve this problem step-by-step. Before giving the final answer, explain your reasoning at each stage. If you're unsure about any step, say so and explain your best guess. [ paste problem here ]"

💡 Forces AI to show its work — dramatically improves accuracy.

Productivity

⚡ Weekly Plan Generator

"Create a focused weekly plan for [GOAL]. My available hours: [X hours/day]. Constraints: [CONSTRAINTS]. Include: daily priorities, time blocks, 2 break activities, and a Friday review checklist. Format as a Monday-Friday table."

💡 Paste into Notion or your calendar app.

Startup

🚀 Startup Idea Validator

"Evaluate this startup idea: [IDEA]. Provide: 1) Problem validation score (1-10), 2) Market size estimate, 3) Top 3 risks, 4) Suggested MVP features, 5) 3 ways to get first 100 users. Be brutally honest — critique the weak points."

💡 The brutal honesty part is crucial. AI defaults to being nice.
