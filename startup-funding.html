Funding Guide

Startup Funding Roadmap

From $0 to IPO. Every funding stage explained with real platforms, free resources, and what you need at each step.

Stage 0

Bootstrapping — $0 to $10K

Start with your own savings, revenue from early customers, or small loans from friends and family. Focus on building an MVP and getting first paying customers.

Indie Hackers Stripe Atlas Gusto Payroll
Pre-Seed $10K — $250K

Pre-Seed Funding

Angel investors, accelerators (YC, Techstars, 500 Startups), and crowdfunding. You need a working prototype and early traction metrics.

Y Combinator Techstars AngelList Kickstarter
Seed $250K — $5M

Seed Round

Seed funds, micro-VCs, and syndicates. You need product-market fit signals: retention, revenue growth, and a clear go-to-market strategy.

Sequoia Scout First Round Better Ventures AngelList Syndicates
Series A $5M — $50M

Series A

Major VC firms. You need proven revenue ($1M+ ARR), strong unit economics, and a scalable business model. This is the make-or-break round.

Benchmark Accel a16z Bessemer
Series B+ $50M+

Series B, C, and Beyond

Growth equity, late-stage VCs, private equity, and eventually IPO. Focus on market expansion, acquisitions, and profitability.

Tiger Global SoftBank Vision NASDAQ IPO Guide
