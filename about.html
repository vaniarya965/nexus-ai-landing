Our Story

About NEXUS Academy

Built by learners, for learners. No barriers. No gatekeeping. Just real education.

NEXUS Academy was created because we believe quality education should never cost money. In a world where AI and tech skills are becoming essential, we noticed a frustrating gap — the best courses are either behind expensive paywalls or buried across hundreds of scattered websites.

So we built NEXUS Academy as a single hub that curates the best free resources from Harvard, Google, freeCodeCamp, Y Combinator, Khan Academy, and dozens more. Every link on this site leads to a real, free course, tool, or template. No affiliate links. No hidden upsells.

🎯 Our Mission

Democratize access to AI, coding, business, and tech skills. We want every student, every entrepreneur, and every career-switcher to have the same learning opportunities as someone with $10,000 to spend on bootcamps. Whether you are 16 or 60, in Mumbai or Montreal — you deserve to learn without paying.

For Students

Learn Python, AI, web development, and data science with free courses from the world's best educators. Take notes, solve quizzes, and build a portfolio of projects — all without spending a rupee.

For Entrepreneurs

Access free business plan templates, pitch deck formats, financial modeling spreadsheets, and startup courses from Y Combinator. Learn digital marketing, AI automation, and how to build a team.

💡 How We Are Different

We are not a course creator — we are a curator and community. We find the best free stuff, organize it, and add tools like notes, quizzes, and progress tracking so you actually finish what you start. Our community forum connects you with mentors and peers. Your dashboard tracks every course you have touched.
