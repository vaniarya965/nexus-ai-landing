Startup Funding Guide

Learn how to raise money, build a pitch deck, find investors, and apply to accelerators. Curated free resources for every stage.

1. Validate Before You Raise

Investors fund traction, not ideas. Use the Business Model Canvas and talk to 20 potential customers before touching a pitch deck.

2. Build Your Pitch Deck

Use the Y Combinator Pitch Deck Template and the Sequoia pitch guide. Free, battle-tested, investor-ready.

3. Financial Model & Cap Table

Download the Wall Street Prep financial model templates and the Carta free cap table tool to show investors you understand your numbers.

4. Find Investors

AngelList, Gust, and LinkedIn are free. Target investors who have funded similar startups. Warm intros > cold emails.

5. Apply to Accelerators

Y Combinator, Techstars, and 500 Startups are free to apply. The application process itself forces you to think clearly about your business.
